About Us

Richfield Graduate Institute of Technology commenced operation in 1990 in Tongaat as a Computer Training Centre. It established branches in various provinces and today has a national footprint.
Richfield operates Higer Education, Technical, Vocational & Further Education Training. Distance Learning and Short Learning Programmes with campuses located across major cities and rural sectors in South Africa.
